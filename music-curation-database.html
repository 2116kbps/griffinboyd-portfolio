Music Curation Database

I was the architect of a MySQL Database and developed a PHP/Javascript/JQuery/AJAX application for R•E•D, a prominent music curation company. R•E•D provides music for major national brands including American Eagle, Harley-Davidson, Ethan Allen, and DSW.

The platform leverages R•E•D's historical curation and data from music APIs to discover new artists, classify genres, retrieve data insights, and automate royalty reporting. The Music Curation Database has become an integral part of the company's operations.
