Cornish Radio

While studying at Cornish College of the Arts in Seattle, I restarted and managed the school's online radio station. Cornish had originally been home to the first college radio program in the country and fostered the creative work of John Cage and Chet Huntley, among others.

I curated and maintained a 24/7 stream of content, selected and trained DJs in broadcasting and royalty reporting, fundraised, and established live video broadcasting from Cornish's concert hall.
