Trimpin + Seattle Symphony

I co-composed a piece for Seattle Symphony’s [untitled] series with Trimpin – the world-renowned kinetic sculptor and MacArthur Genius. I wrote all of the music for the symphony musicians, and prepared and formatted scores and parts. I assisted with installation of the robotic instruments, and I also built a video projection system in MAX Jitter to showcase live feeds of the instruments during the performance.

I also co-produced an audio recording for digital release and a limited pressing of vinyl LPs.
