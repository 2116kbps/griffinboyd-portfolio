Rhythm Designer

The Rhythm Designer is an advanced step sequencer built in MAX/MSP. Beyond having the capabilities of standard drum machines, the Rhythm Designer's strength is creating subdivisions and polyrhythms. Users can build their own rhythms, or ask the program to generate rhythms based on chosen constraints.

I am continuing to develop and modify this technology along with other live performance tools for intuitive complex improvisation.
